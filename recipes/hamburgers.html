Hamburgers

Hamburgers are a classic American favorite, featuring juicy beef patties served in a bun with your choice of toppings. Perfect for cookouts and casual meals.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef (80/20 blend recommended)
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 slices cheddar cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles (for topping)
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise (for serving)

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into 4 equal patties. Season both sides with salt and pepper.
  3. Cook the patties for 3-4 minutes per side, or until desired doneness.
  4. Add cheese slices during the last minute of cooking, if using.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster.
  6. Assemble the burgers with your favorite toppings and condiments.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Enjoy your homemade hamburgers!
