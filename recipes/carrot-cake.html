Carrot Cake

Carrot cake is a moist and flavorful dessert made with grated carrots, warm spices, and a rich cream cheese frosting. It's a favorite for celebrations and gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups grated carrots
  • 1 1/2 cups sugar
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts (optional)
  • Cream cheese frosting

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x13 inch baking pan.
  2. In a large bowl, beat together the sugar, oil, eggs, and vanilla until smooth.
  3. In another bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.
  5. Fold in the grated carrots and walnuts.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and spread evenly.
  7. Bake for 35-40 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Let the cake cool completely before frosting with cream cheese frosting.

Enjoy your homemade carrot cake!
