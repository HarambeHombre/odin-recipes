Shish Kabob

Shish kabob is a popular Middle Eastern dish featuring skewered and grilled cubes of meat and vegetables. It's perfect for barbecues and outdoor gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound beef sirloin or chicken breast, cut into 1-inch cubes
  • 1 red bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 green bell pepper, cut into chunks
  • 1 red onion, cut into chunks
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Wooden or metal skewers

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, salt, and pepper.
  2. Add the meat cubes and toss to coat. Marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Thread the meat and vegetables alternately onto skewers.
  4. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  5. Grill the kabobs for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until the meat is cooked through and vegetables are tender.
  6. Serve hot with rice or flatbread.

Enjoy your homemade shish kabob!
